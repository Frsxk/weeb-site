Anime

Anime refers to animated TV shows, films, or web series. Anime can cover a wide range of genres and target various age groups, from children to adults. The animation can be hand-drawn or created using computer-generated imagery (CGI). Anime often adapts manga or light novels but can also be original works.

Anime Category

Manga

Manga refers to Japanese comic books or graphic novels. Manga is typically serialized in magazines before being compiled into tankobon volumes. It covers a diverse array of genres and caters to various demographics, including shonen (targeted at young males), shoujo (targeted at young females), seinen (targeted at adult males), and josei (targeted at adult females).
