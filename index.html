Websites to Watch Anime Legally

You should avoid pirated anime because it's against the law.
Any websites that streams pirated anime will be taken down by the government as soon as possible.

Bstation/Bilibili

Bilibili is a website for sharing videos with a focus on animation, comics, and games, based in China. Users can submit, watch, and add subtitled comments to the videos.

Bilibili

Netflix

Netflix boasts a vast collection of titles, spanning from dramas, films, and documentaries to anime. In recent years, Netflix has also expanded its repertoire of original content to make the platform even more exclusive.

Netflix

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is one of the largest anime streaming sites, offering over 850 titles. Beyond anime, users can also watch Japanese dramas (dorama) or read manga (Japanese comics) on Crunchyroll.

Crunchyroll
