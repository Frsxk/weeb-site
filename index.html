Websites to Watch Anime/Read Manga Legally

You should avoid pirated anime/manga because it's against the law.
Any websites that streams pirated anime/manga will be taken down by the government as soon as possible.

Bstation/Bilibili

Bilibili is a website for sharing videos with a focus on animation, comics, and games, based in China. Users can submit, watch, and add subtitled comments to the videos.

Bilibili

MANGA Plus

MANGA Plus by Shueisha is an online manga platform and smartphone app owned by Shueisha that was launched on January 28, 2019. It is available worldwide except in Japan, China, and South Korea which already have their own services.

Manga Plus

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is one of the largest anime streaming sites, offering over 850 titles. Beyond anime, users can also watch Japanese dramas (dorama) or read manga (Japanese comics) on Crunchyroll.

Crunchyroll
